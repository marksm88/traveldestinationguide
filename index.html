Main Topic

The main topic of this project is to create a comprehensive travel guide website.

Objectives

The main objectives of the site are to provide detailed information about various travel destinations, offer travel tips and recommendations, and inspire visitors to explore new places.

Target Audience

The target audience includes travelers of all ages and backgrounds who are seeking information and inspiration for their next journey. This determination was made based on the broad appeal of travel-related content and the desire to cater to both novice and seasoned travelers.

Content

The website will provide detailed destination guides, including information on attractions, local culture, cuisine, transportation, and accommodation options. Additionally, there will be general travel tips, packing advice, and travel-related articles to enhance the visitor's travel experience.

Design Ideas

Initial design concepts include a clean and user-friendly interface with intuitive navigation, high-quality imagery of destinations, and a responsive layout that adapts well to various devices. These design fundamentals aim to create an engaging and visually appealing experience that aligns with the goals of inspiring travel and providing practical information.

HTML & CSS Usage

HTML will be used for structuring the content of the website, ensuring that information is organized logically and accessible to users and search engines. CSS will be employed for presentation purposes, including layout, colors, fonts, and overall styling, to enhance the visual appeal and user experience of the site.
